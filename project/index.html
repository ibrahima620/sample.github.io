History

The history of AI dates back to ancient times, with myths and rumors of artificial beings. However, it was after World War II and the invention of programmable computers that AI began to flourish. The 1956 Dartmouth Conference established AI as a full-fledged scientific discipline.

Recent Advancements

Over the past few decades, AI has made spectacular progress thanks to deep learning, increased available data, and the use of graphics processors. The transformer architecture has also revolutionized the field.
